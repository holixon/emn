---
title: Motivation and Goal
---

The goal of EMN is to provide a formal yet practical notation for Event Modeling.
Event Modeling has proven useful for designing systems by making workflows, data, and interactions visible.
However, until now it lacked a formal notation suitable for exchange, tooling, and automation.
EMN addresses this gap by defining a precise, XML-based specification inspired by BPMN, enriched with Diagram Interchange (DI) for visualization.
The motivation is to provide a standard that supports collaboration across stakeholders, from business experts to developers.
This section explains why EMN exists and what value it brings to modern system design practices.
